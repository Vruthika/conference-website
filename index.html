About the Conference

The National Conference on Innovative Trends in Technologies (NCITT 2025) is a premier academic conference that brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to share their innovative ideas and research findings in the field of emerging technologies.

This conference provides an excellent platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration among participants from various institutions and organizations. The conference aims to highlight the latest technological advancements and their applications in solving real-world problems.

About KEC

Kongu Engineering College, established in 1983, stands as a premier educational institution affiliated to Anna University Chennai. Located on a sprawling 167-acre campus in Erode District, Tamil Nadu, KEC has emerged as a leader in professional and career-oriented education.

The institution offers cutting-edge programs at the intersection of theory and practice, fostering an environment of rigorous scholarship and innovation. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and excellent academic records, KEC has earned recognition as one of India's reputed educational institutions.

About CSI

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. Formed in 1965, CSI has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path since its formative years.

The CSI - KEC Student Branch actively organizes technical events, workshops, and conferences to enhance students' technical knowledge and professional skills. Through various activities, it provides a platform for students to learn, interact, and grow in the field of computer science and technology.
